Introducing MoM School

MoM School is the foundation of Subha-artha. Here, we teach four essentials:

  1. Basic English & Communication
  2. Basic Science (up to Class 10)
  3. Economy (Savings & Investment)
  4. Yoga & Diet

Just like a mother who never gave up on us—even when teachers said, “Your child is too naughty, I can’t teach him alphabets and numbers”—our mothers stood with unmatched patience and sacrifice to teach us the very basics.

So, to all mothers out there—please give MoM School a chance to fulfill not just our dream, but your dream too.
